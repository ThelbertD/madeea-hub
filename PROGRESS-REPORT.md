# MadeEA Command Center — Progress Update

**Prepared for:** Client review
**Status:** Live build, in active development

---

## Where we are

The MadeEA Command Center is **up and running** as a real, branded web app — your navy-and-orange identity and logo throughout. It's a secure, cloud-based hub where an EA can run their whole day from one screen.

## What's working today

- **Dashboard** — an at-a-glance command center: active tasks, today's meetings, pending emails, and running automations.
- **Task Manager** — a drag-and-drop board (To Do / In Progress / Done) with add, edit, due dates, and priorities. Everything saves automatically.
- **Client Vault** — full profiles for every client (preferences, tone, notes, tasks, schedule), with add and edit.
- **Communication Center** — inbox triage with one-click **AI-drafted replies**.
- **AI Suite** — *Communication Studio* (emails, reports, proposals, press releases) and *Bookkeeping AI* (invoices, expense reports, budgets). Output is clean and formatted, with **one-click PDF export**.
- **AI Quick Actions** — instant one-click AI generators, plus a shortcut panel on every page.
- **AI Assistant** — a 24/7 chat helper.
- **Search & notifications** — find any client, task, or message instantly; get alerted to urgent items.
- **Integrations** — connects to **Gmail and Google Calendar** (live), with **Slack** support.

## How sign-in works

The app is **invite-only** — it's an internal tool for your team, not a public sign-up site. For now, **self-registration is turned off**; new team members are simply **added by an administrator** through the back office. This keeps access tight and secure while we finalize the team-management screen.

## What's next

- A built-in screen for admins to invite and manage EAs (so accounts can be added without the back office).
- Final connection of the AI engine and a few polish items.
- Optional: scheduled automations and deeper Slack features.
